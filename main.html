EPA estimates that more food reaches landfills and combustion facilities than any other single material in our everyday trash, constituting 24 percent of the amount of municipal solid waste (MSW) landfilled and 22 percent of the amount of MSW combusted with energy recovery.. -> By improving composting ID at the consumer level & sorting in processing facilities, we reduce landfill & Greenhouse gas emissions ( Explanation: Since food is a major contributor to the amount of methane generated by and released from landfills, and since landfills are one of the top sources of methane emissions, taking action on wasted food will help reduce the amount of methane that is released.)
